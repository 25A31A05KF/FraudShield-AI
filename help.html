Help & Support

We are here to assist you in dealing with online frauds, scams, and cybercrime. If you encounter suspicious messages, transactions, or any digital threats, follow the guidance below to protect yourself and report the issue.

Cyber Crime Helpline

You can report cybercrime incidents directly to the national cybercrime helpline. Reach out via the official portal for assistance:

Report at Cyber Crime Helpline

Steps to Get Support

1. Stay Calm: Do not panic. Take note of all suspicious activity.

2. Collect Information: Screenshot messages, note transaction IDs, and save emails or links.

3. Report Immediately: Contact the cybercrime helpline or local authorities to report the incident.

4. Avoid Sharing Personal Details: Never share OTPs, passwords, or account information with unknown sources.
